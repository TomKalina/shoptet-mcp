# List recent orders

> "Show me the latest orders"

Uses `list_orders` to display recent orders with their status, customer info, and totals.

## What you can ask

- "Show me orders from this week"
- "List orders with status 'shipped'"
- "How many orders came in today?"
- "Show me unpaid orders"

## Tool used

`list_orders` with optional filters: `statusId`, `creationTimeFrom`, `creationTimeTo`, `code`

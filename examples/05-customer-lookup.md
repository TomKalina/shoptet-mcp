# Look up customer details

> "Find customer with email john@example.com"

Uses `list_customers` with email filter to find a customer, then `get_customer` for full details.

## What you can ask

- "Who is customer john@example.com?"
- "List all customers registered this month"
- "Show me details for customer GUID xyz"

## Tools used

- `list_customers` - search by email or creation date
- `get_customer` - get full customer profile

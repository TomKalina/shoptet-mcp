# Check stock levels

> "What's my current stock?"

Uses `list_products` and `get_stock` to check inventory levels across all products and warehouses.

## What you can ask

- "Which products are out of stock?"
- "What's the stock level for Ardbeg Ten?"
- "Show me products with less than 5 items in stock"

## Tools used

- `list_products` - get all products
- `get_stock` - check stock levels for a specific product
